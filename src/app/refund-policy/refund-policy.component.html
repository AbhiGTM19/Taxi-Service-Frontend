Refund Policy At RV Taxi Services, we strive to provide the best possible experience for our customers. We understand that sometimes plans change, and you may need to cancel your booking. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds.

1. Cancellation by Customer

Cancellation Made More Than 24 Hours Before Pickup: If you cancel your booking more than 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time, you will be eligible for a full refund.
Cancellation Made Less Than 24 Hours Before Pickup: If you cancel your booking less than 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time, you will not be eligible for a refund.

2. No-Show Policy

If you do not show up for your scheduled pickup and do not cancel in advance, you will not be eligible for a refund.

3. Cancellation by RV Taxi Services

In the unlikely event that we need to cancel your booking, you will receive a full refund. We will notify you as soon as possible and assist you in making alternative arrangements.

4. Refund Process

How to Request a Refund: To request a refund, please contact our customer service team at rvtaxiservices620@gmail.com or +91-9201709650 with your booking details.
Refund Timeline: Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days from the date of the cancellation or refund request.
Refund Method: Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used at the time of booking.

5. Non-Refundable Services

Special Promotions: Bookings made under special promotions or discounts may not be eligible for a refund. Please refer to the terms and conditions of the specific promotion for details.
Additional Services: Any additional services (e.g., special requests, add-ons) are non-refundable once confirmed.

6. Disputes

If you believe you are entitled to a refund that has not been processed, please contact our customer service team. We will review your case and respond within 10 business days.

7. Changes to This Refund Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
